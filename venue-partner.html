Cloth & Flame is an experiential event company creating boundary-pushing, connection-inspiring encounters wherever they can be imagined. Through a growing portfolio of stunning properties and wild landscapes, culinary programming, planning, design, and rentals, a devoted team crafts bespoke private and corporate events, weddings, community dinners, and intriguing activations. We elevate ideas into creative visions, celebrate and steward treasured places, and create everlasting connections between lands, communities, and people.
